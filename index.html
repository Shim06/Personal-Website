About me

Growing up, I was almost always inside because I lived in a city, and because of that I played various games. My first handheld was a PSP, my first console was a PS3, and I spent hundreds of hours on my DS. My inclination to games naturally made me interested in technology as a whole. I would be curious with how hardware and software both worked together to create the games that I loved to play. I had always loved problem solving, and as such, seeing that I could create code and programs that could solve problems and automate tasks, this would spark my love for programming and Computer Science in general. Now, I am continually learning more about Computer Science and making projects that interests me in my free time.
